The Story of Butler@Her

Butler@Her was founded with a single vision: to create a safe, empowering, and dependable transportation service designed specifically for women, by women.

Our founders recognized a major gap in the taxi and ride-sharing industry—where safety, comfort, and understanding were not guaranteed for female passengers. What began as a community-led initiative quickly transformed into a full-fledged transportation company focused on dignity, trust, and solidarity.

With a fleet of licensed female drivers and a mission to connect women safely across cities, Butler@Her is more than a ride—it's a movement. We’re proud to redefine the ride experience through compassion, professionalism, and empowerment.
